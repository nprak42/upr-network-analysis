About This Research

This project analyzes collaboration networks among civil society organizations participating in the UN Universal Periodic Review. By examining joint submissions, we identify key organizations, brokerage positions, and patterns of advocacy coalition formation.

Methods: Network analysis using NetworkX, visualization with PyVis, metrics include degree centrality, betweenness centrality, structural constraint (brokerage), and community detection.

Research by Niranjan Prakash | University of Massachusetts Amherst
